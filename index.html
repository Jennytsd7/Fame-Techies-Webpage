Chocolates, anybody?


Chocolates are meant to mend your mood and your day too! That's the inspiration behind the inception of Delecta. Homemaker turned artisan chocolatier Sreevidya Rao and her husband Koteshwar Rao started off on a humble journey under the brand name Chocoluk, to bring happiness to people by blending chocolates with art. After receiving immense appreciation for their work, the duo decided to go big under the brand name Delecta - Distinctive flavors with a dash of love in every box. Furthermore, a fully-equipped workshop with efficient employees to manage, deliver and administer the business was setup.
